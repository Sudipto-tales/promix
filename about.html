Who We Are

Who is Promix?

We are a marketing and technology company that helps businesses grow their brand and build the tech behind it. Strength in strategy, power in progress.

Our Journey

The Promix Story

2020

Founded with a Vision

Promix was born from a simple idea: businesses deserve a partner that understands both marketing and technology. We started with a small team and big ambitions.

2021

First 10 Clients

Word spread quickly. Within our first full year, we partnered with 10 clients across marketing, web development, and brand strategy.

2022

Expanded to Tech Services

We launched our full-stack product engineering division, building web and mobile applications alongside our marketing work.

2023

30+ Projects Completed

A milestone year. We delivered over 30 projects, from enterprise software to viral marketing campaigns, establishing ourselves as a versatile partner.

2024

AI & Automation Launched

We added AI engineering and intelligent automation to our service lineup, helping clients leverage machine learning, NLP, and generative AI.

2025

Growing Global Reach

Today, Promix serves clients across industries and geographies, with a growing team of marketers, designers, and engineers building the future together.
